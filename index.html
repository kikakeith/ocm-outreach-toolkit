Operator Trading Cards

Collectible-style cards featuring licensed cannabis operators in LA County. Click to flip and learn more about each operator.

Jason McKnight

Green Label RX • CEO Farmer

Social-equity operator who founded Green Label Rx after years as a legacy cultivator. Survived the War on Drugs, went from incarceration to operating large-scale cultivation and manufacturing. His story reflects resilience and the possibilities created through social equity programs.

Download

Maisha Bahati

Crystal Nugs • CEO Retail + Delivery

CEO of Crystal Nugs, Sacramento's first Black- and woman-owned cannabis delivery service. Opened the city's first Black-woman owned dispensary in 2023, expanding from delivery to a flagship Midtown store.

Download

Angie Macaraeg

Encore Labs • VP Sales & Marketing Testing

VP of Sales and Marketing at Encore Labs, where she helped build California's statewide cannabis testing standards. Brings deep expertise in lab compliance, product safety, and quality assurance.

Download

Christopher Martinez

Sensi • CEO Retail

CEO of Sensi, a retail dispensary in Canoga Park. Brings deep expertise in cannabis licensing and regulatory compliance, having worked on the first Jeeter facility in Desert Hot Springs before launching his own operation.

Download

Madison Shockley III

CADRE • CEO Retail

CEO of CADRE, a social-equity company in South LA combining retail, culture, and advocacy. Champions "The People, The Culture, The Revolution" while building a brand that celebrates Black joy and resilience.

Download

Luis Rivera

Bonafide Delivery • CEO Delivery

Founder of Collective Strategies Enterprises and GreenWay HighWay. Verified Social Equity Applicant in LA. Founded Social Equity LA in 2019 providing free resources. Launched the Cannabis Delivery Network in 2021.

Download

Victoria Basso

AJ's Cannabis Co. • Ops Manager MicroBiz

Partner and Operations Manager at AJ's Cannabis Company with a background in lab testing and compliance. Created Colors of Cannabis, a podcast spotlighting women and BIPOC contributors.

Download

Sakara Barnes

Mother Exotic Flower • CEO Cultivation

CEO of Mother Exotic Flower, known for cultivating premium flower with a deep commitment to community care. Building a cultivation brand rooted in quality and social responsibility in Los Angeles.

Download

Ramon Garcia & Nina Parks

Equity Trade Certification • Founders Distribution

Social-equity operators and supply-chain builders. Co-creators of the National Equity Trade Certification. Second-generation cultivators helping certified equity brands reach retail shelves.

Download

Tianna Woodruff

Queen Mary • CEO Manufacturing

Founder and CEO of Queen Mary, designing daily-use cannabis for women's wellness. Started as an LA delivery driver in 2016, built a brand after not being selected for retail. Expanded to Colorado in 2025.

Download

Rhavin McSweaney

Dope Sugar • Founder Retail

Founder of Dope Sugar, among the few Black woman dispensary owners in Los Angeles. Building a retail brand rooted in community representation and equity since 2018.
